Ana's Art Dolls — Georgian Collection

Each doll is a small, original piece of art — shaped slowly by hand with patience and heart. The collection gathers characters and costumes touched by Georgian craft, folk colour, and a playful sense of story.

Inspired by traditions yet open to imagination, these figures carry texture, embroidery, and detail meant to feel personal and alive on the shelf or in the hand.

Custom orders

Custom handmade dolls are also available by request. Each doll can be created with different clothing styles, inspired by Georgian traditions or designed according to your personal wishes.

Traditional dress, contemporary outfits, or a fully bespoke look — share your idea and we can shape a one-of-a-kind companion together.

Veil & Silver

A fully handmade doll in traditional Georgian spirit: long braids crowned with a patterned silver band and a soft sheer veil, a tailored black jacket over a long deep-blue skirt. The centre panel and hem are touched with hand-finished silver motifs and a slim belt for an elegant, ceremonial look.

Crimson & Lace

Bold black, red, and white balance in this one-of-a-kind piece: a structured folk hat with red accents, a beaded necklace, wide black sleeves trimmed in sparkling red, and a full white skirt — every layer stitched and finished by hand for a proud, festive character.
